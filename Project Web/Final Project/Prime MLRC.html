About Us

MATHEMA LEARNING RESOURCE CENTER

Late Prof. Dr. Sundar Shyam Bhakta Mathema

Niraj Thapa Magar

MLRC Co-ordinator

Named after the founder and former Chairperson of Prime Educational Inc. Late Prof. Dr. Sundar Shyam Bhakta Mathema, the Mathema Learning Resource Centre was founded to streamline and modernize the learning process for all undergraduate and graduate students at Prime College. The board objective of MLRC is to fill the gap between prescribed coursework and real-world requirements. In order to achieve this goal, we offer our students various value-added courses, workshops and training.

Student Clubs

Prime Student Clubs provide a platform to the students which helps them enhance their skills and guides them in their journey.
Clubs in Prime College are all about boosting students' confidence, personality, leadership, and pace for moving forward in the future.

Prime Sports Club

"Friendship through Sportsmanship"

The Prime sports club is a group of sporty and dedicated students, aiming to cultivate students' talents in various sports, help them master sports abilities, and develop their comprehensive personality. The club provides the platform for the students and teachers to enhance their abilities by letting them indulge in sports events.

Learn More

Prime Cultural Club

"We Before I"

Established in 2003, Prime Cultural Club is a team of enthusiastic students with the vision We Before I, formed to raise cultural awareness among the students.

Learn More

Prime IT Club

Prime IT Club is a group of creative and tech-inspired students dedicated to empowering IT knowledge. Understanding the current market demands, the club focuses on developing IT skills among the students through different workshops, seminars, and training programs.

Learn More

Club 25 Prime Chapter

Club 25 Prime Chapter is a youthful concept based on the same values and norms as Club 25 Nepal. The club helps the students develop a positive attitude and focuses on making them understand the value and importance of community well-being through various social campaigns. It promotes blood donation and urges people to save lives by donating blood.

Learn More

Prime Entrepreneurial Management Club

"It’s we building us."

Prime EMC is a team of determined and aspiring students, moving forward with the motto; It’s we building us. The club works together to promote entrepreneurship, leadership, management and networking skills in and among students. Today, in addition to entrepreneurship and leadership skills, Prime EMC is highly inclined towards event management, collaboration and networking!

Learn More

Research Department

The Research Department at Prime plays a vital role in the further development of academic research in the field of business, management, and IT.
The objective of the Research Department is to establish a research culture in Nepal by supporting and providing a platform for students, faculty members and the staffs for their research project and activities.

Student Support Division

Student Support Division provides services and programs that address academic, behavioral, social, emotional and health needs of all students at Prime.
One of the main objectives of the Student Support division is to provide direct services to the students within our college, especially those who are experiencing a problem that creates a barrier to learning.
